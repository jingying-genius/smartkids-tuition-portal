## How to See Each View

### **Click the buttons at the top to switch between views:**

1. **Application Form** (Active by default)
   - The colorful registration form parents fill out
   - Has all fields: parent info, student info, class selection, household income, etc.

2. **Admin Review** (Click "Admin Review" button)
   - Church staff dashboard to review applications
   - Shows table of all applications with:
     - App ID, student name, grade, classes, household income
     - Filter by status (New/Reviewing/Approved/Rejected)
     - Quick action buttons to review each application
     - Click the ğŸ‘ï¸ icon to open detailed review modal
   - Stats: New Applications, Under Review, Approved, Rejected

3. **Approval Email** (Click "Approval Email" button)
   - Shows what the email looks like that parents receive
   - Includes: enrollment details, payment info, visitation leader contact
   - Has "Make Payment Now" button

4. **Payment** (Click "Payment" button)
   - Payment interface parents see
   - Shows: payment summary, amount due, payment methods
   - Options: PayNow (with QR code), Credit Card, Bank Transfer
   - Clean, direct payment flow (no QR maze!)

5. **Parent Dashboard** (Click "Parent Dashboard" button)
   - What parents see after enrollment
   - Shows: enrollment status, upcoming classes, visitation leader contact

6. **Leader View** (Click "Leader View" button)
   - Visitation leader dashboard
   - Stats for their zone (total students, pending, payment required, active)
   - List of all students in their zone with:
     - Approval status
     - Payment status
     - Fee status (full/waiver/partial)
     - Parent contact info
     - Action buttons (View Details, Message, etc.)
